The Wedding Events

Saturday, July 11, 2015

Join us for the Wedding Ceremony & Reception at the beautiful Rosemont Historic Manor, located in Berryville, Virginia.

The address to the venue is:
16 Rosemont Manor Lane, Berryville, VA 22611.

The Main Ceremony

5:30 PM - 6:00 PM
The Ceremony itself will begin at 5:30 PM & will be held on the front lawn of The Manor. It will be followed by a Happy Hour & a rockin' Party that will last the rest of the evening.
Learn More

The Reception

6:00 PM - 11:00 PM
The Reception will begin immediately following the Ceremony & will be held at "The Carriage House", which is just a short walk from the location of the Main Ceremony.
Learn More
